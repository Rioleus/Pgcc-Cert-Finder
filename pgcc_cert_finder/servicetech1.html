Computer Service Technology Certificate

It seems that you're a few courses away from getting this certificate.
You have to choose 1 Elective between these courses:

  • INT-1450 CCNA 1: Intro to Cisco Network
  • INT-1460 CCNA 2: Routing and Switching Essentials
  • INT-1680 Ethical Hacking and Network Defense I
  • INT-2300 Windows Desktop Client
  • INT-2530 Telecommunications
  • INT-2910 Cooperative Education
  • INT-2920 Cooperative Education
  • INT-2930 Cooperative Education

This program prepares students for computer support careers.
It also prepares students for the CompTIA A+ computer support certification, the CompTIA Network + certification, and the CompTIA Security+ certification.
