Electronics Analysis and Repair Certificate

It seems that you're less than a few courses away from getting this certificate.
Those courses are ENT-2200 High Reliability Soldering and Fabrication, ENT-2710 Analog Circuits, and ENT-1850 Circuits Evaluation and Repair.

Students who complete this program should have the ability to analyze complex electronics systems, both analog and digital.
They also should have the hands-on skills needed to repair electronic devices.
