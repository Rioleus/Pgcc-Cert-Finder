IET Certificate finder

Welcome to Prince George's Community College Certificate finder for IET Certificates.The Information and Engineering Technology (IET) department offers quality instruction through comprehensive degree and certificate options in the areas of programming, computer networking, Web technology, and systems engineering/analysis.
It also provides opportunities for all students at the college to gain technology fluency through its general education offering, Introduction to Information Technology.
IET faculty facilitate innovative learning experiences that equip students with the technical and problem-solving competencies required to obtain employment in the computer industry or continue education at a four-year institution.
In addition, the faculty work diligently to create partnerships with local technology employers and are active participants in private and government technology initiatives to ensure currency in both instructional methods and curricula. The department also provides student support mechanisms and dynamic programs that foster retention.
With this site, students will be able to select which courses they have taken, and determine which certifications they qualify for. It will also let students know if they are only 1 course short of a certification. If so they can take the course or see the department chair to inquire about a possible substitution. The IET department consists of Information Technology, Construction Management and Engineering Technology.
